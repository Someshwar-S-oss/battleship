AUDIO FILES
===========

Place your audio files here for background music and sound effects.

BACKGROUND MUSIC - 4 TRACKS (Optional)
---------------------------------------
The game uses different music for each game state:

1. MENU MUSIC
   - menu.ogg (recommended), menu.mp3, or menu.wav
   - Plays: Main menu and settings screen
   - Loops: Yes
   - Style: Upbeat, inviting

2. BATTLE MUSIC
   - battle.ogg (recommended), battle.mp3, or battle.wav
   - Plays: Ship placement and combat
   - Loops: Yes
   - Style: Tense, dramatic naval/war theme

3. VICTORY MUSIC
   - victory.ogg (recommended), victory.mp3, or victory.wav
   - Plays: When you win
   - Loops: No (plays once)
   - Style: Triumphant, celebratory

4. DEFEAT MUSIC
   - defeat.ogg (recommended), defeat.mp3, or defeat.wav
   - Plays: When you lose
   - Loops: No (plays once)
   - Style: Somber, reflective

Format: OGG (best), MP3, or WAV
Duration: Loop-friendly for menu/battle (3-5 minutes)
Duration: Shorter for victory/defeat (30-90 seconds)

SOUND EFFECTS (Optional)
-------------------------
- hit.wav or hit.ogg - Played when a shot hits a ship
- miss.wav or miss.ogg - Played when a shot misses
- sink.wav or sink.ogg - Played when a ship is completely sunk

Format: WAV or OGG preferred
Duration: Short (0.5-2 seconds)
Style: 
  * Hit: Explosion, impact sound
  * Miss: Water splash
  * Sink: Larger explosion or sinking sound

The game will work without these files (silent mode).
You can find free sound effects and music at:
- freesound.org
- opengameart.org
- incompetech.com (music)
- zapsplat.com
